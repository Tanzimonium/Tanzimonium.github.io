Welcome Week 2024

Congratulations on making it to Warwick!

Come and find us at our stall on the Fresher's Fair on Thursday 26/7. Afterwards you can find us at our first Happy Lemon trip of the academic year at 6.30pm in Cannon Park. Buy some boba and have a chat or meet us later on in OC0.1 in the Oculus from 7.00pm onwards for some chill socialising and games.

We will also be hosting a picnic on Saturday 28/7 on the Oculus Field from 1.00pm to 6.00pm. Drop by whenever! There will be games and people to meet.

Our executive team will be around in pink hoodies, feel free to come and talk to us. We are happy to answer any questions you may have!
